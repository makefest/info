# Creating Outlook accounts for kids

- reminder: when you have created an Outlook account, you automatically have a Microsoft account
- sign in to your Microsoft account by going to [account.microsoft.com](https://account.microsoft.com) or shorter to [live.com](https://live.com)
- on the main page, search the "Family" area and click on "View your family"
- alternatively, you can also visit [family.microsoft.com](https://family.microsoft.com) directly
- click on "Add a family member" and follow the questions, choosing the option to create a new e-mail account; my recommendation is to choose outlook.com as the e-mail ending (domain)
- it is important to note the passwords safely, so that you can give them to the kids later on
- I recommend creating two accounts which just means going through the process twice
  - for one of the accounts I recommend using the name, so for instance donald.duck@outlook.com
  - of course it's possible that an address is already taken; in that case, just try variations until you find an available address
  - for the other account I recommend a name like donald_adm@outlook.com, since this account is meant for administrative tasks only; again, if needed, modify the address until you find an available one
